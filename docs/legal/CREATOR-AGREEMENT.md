# Creator Revenue Share Agreement

**Last Updated:** June 12, 2026

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Wizard-AI S.r.l.** ("Company", "we") and the Developer ("Creator", "you") who submits and publishes a Skill in the Wizard-AI ecosystem.

## 1. Object of the Agreement
This Agreement governs the way you publish your Skill in the Wizard-AI hub and defines the terms for your participation in our Revenue Share program.
Wizard-AI's goal is to allow creators to earn **passive royalties** based on the usage of their Skills by AI agents.

## 2. Nature of the Relationship
This Agreement constitutes a software use license with a royalty clause and, where applicable, an independent contractor agreement.
**There is no relationship of subordination, employment, or agency between you and Wizard-AI.** You are free to set your own hours, your development methods, and to collaborate with other projects.

## 3. Intellectual Property
- You retain exclusive ownership of the copyright and all intellectual property rights to the original code you develop for your Skill.
- By submitting the Skill via Pull Request, you grant Wizard-AI a perpetual, worldwide, commercial license in accordance with the **[Contributor License Agreement (CLA)](../../CLA.md)**.

## 4. Royalty Model ("Spotify for AI")

### A. Revenue Calculation
- Wizard-AI collects revenue from the sale of Enterprise Licenses to companies and from potential donations/sponsorships (the "Revenue Pool").
- We collect anonymous statistical data (opt-in telemetry) to determine how many times your Skill is invoked compared to the total ("Usage Share").

### B. Distribution and Percentages
- **Creator Pool (60-70%):** Of the *net* company revenue (after taxes, Stripe payment fees, and refunds), a share between 60% and 70% is allocated to the Creator Pool.
- **Your Royalties:** You will receive a percentage of the Creator Pool proportional to your Skill's Usage Share in the reference period.

### C. Payment Terms
- Payments ("Payouts") are made **quarterly**.
- Minimum threshold: €50.00. If your royalties in a quarter do not reach this threshold, they will roll over to the next quarter.
- All payouts are securely managed via **Stripe Connect**. You must complete the onboarding process (KYC) on Stripe to receive funds.
- You are solely responsible for declaring income and paying any applicable taxes on your royalties in your country of tax residence.

## 5. Transparency and Audit
Through the "Creator Dashboard" in the Wizard-AI Hub, you will have access to estimated metrics and aggregated data on your Skill's usage.
You have the right to request clarification on royalty calculations. Wizard-AI commits to providing transparent reporting at each payment cycle.

## 6. Quality and Skill Removal
- Wizard-AI reserves the right to test, approve, or reject any Skill before publication in the Marketplace to ensure the security and stability of the ecosystem.
- You may request the removal of your Skill from the official Marketplace at any time. However, the license granted for versions already distributed to customers (as per the CLA) remains valid, and you will continue to earn royalties for the use of those versions, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## 7. Jurisdiction
This Agreement is governed by Italian law. In the event of disputes that cannot be resolved amicably, exclusive jurisdiction shall lie with the Courts of the registered office of Wizard-AI S.r.l.

To accept this Agreement, simply submit the Pull Request containing your Skill and activate your Creator profile in the Hub.
