## 2. Types of Data Collected and Purposes

We collect personal data strictly necessary for the functioning of the ecosystem and the provision of our services.

### A. Local Hub Users (Community)
Wizard-AI Hub operates entirely locally. By default, **no data is transmitted to our servers**.
- **Opt-in Telemetry:** Only if the User provides explicit consent, we collect anonymous usage data (invoked skill, timestamp, anonymous instance identifier) for the sole purpose of calculating royalties for Creators. *No prompts, source code, or personal data are transmitted.*
  - **Legal Basis:** Consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR).

### B. Creators (Skill Developers)
For Creators who join the Revenue Share program:
- **Data collected:** Name, email, GitHub username, tax and billing information.
- **Purposes:** Management of royalty payouts, service communications, tax and contractual obligations.
- **Legal Basis:** Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR) and Legal obligation (Art. 6.1.c GDPR).

### C. Enterprise Customers
For corporate contacts purchasing the Premium/API version:
- **Data collected:** Personal details, corporate email, role, payment data.
- **Purposes:** Provision of the SaaS service, invoicing, customer support.
- **Legal Basis:** Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).

## 5. Data Subject Rights

Pursuant to Articles 15-22 of the GDPR, the User has the right to:
1. **Access:** Obtain confirmation of the existence of personal data and request a copy.
2. **Rectification:** Update or correct inaccurate data.
3. **Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** Request the deletion of data (if no retention obligations exist).
4. **Restriction / Objection:** Object to processing or request its restriction.
5. **Portability:** Receive their data in a structured, machine-readable format.
6. **Withdraw Consent:** Remove telemetry opt-in at any time (via the Wizard-AI Hub interface).

To exercise these rights, write to: privacy@wizard-ai.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali)** (www.garanteprivacy.it) or your local EU supervisory authority.
