# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** June 12, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Wizard-AI ("we", "our", or "the Company") uses cookies and similar technologies within the Wizard-AI Hub and our official website.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer or mobile device) when you visit a website or use a web app. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over time.

## How we use cookies in Wizard-AI Hub (Local App)

Since Wizard-AI Hub is primarily an application that runs locally on your computer, the use of cookies and storage technologies is extremely limited and aimed solely at improving your user experience.

We do not use profiling or advertising tracking cookies.

We primarily use your browser's **Local Storage** for the following purposes:

### 1. Strictly Necessary Storage
This data is essential for the Hub to function. It cannot be disabled.
- **OAuth Session Data:** Temporary tokens used to maintain the login to your Creator account via GitHub.
- **Interface State:** Minimal visual preferences to ensure the local app loads correctly (e.g., open tabs).

### 2. Functional and Preference Storage
- **Telemetry Preference (`wizard_telemetry_optin`):** Stores your choice (opt-in or opt-out) regarding the transmission of anonymous skill usage data.
- **Anonymous Instance Identifier (`wizard_instance_id`):** A randomly generated local string used *only* if you have enabled telemetry, to group events anonymously without identifying you personally.

### 3. Chat Storage
- **Chat History:** Messages exchanged with the local AI assistant are temporarily saved in Local Storage so you don't lose the conversation if you reload the page. This data is never sent to our servers.

## Managing Preferences

You can delete all locally stored data from Wizard-AI Hub at any time:
1. Within the Hub, by going to Settings and clicking "Clear Local Data".
2. Directly from your web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox) by clearing cache and site data for `localhost`.
3. From the terminal by running the command: `ai-hub --delete-data`.

## Third-Party Cookies

If you access our public website (different from the local Hub) or use the Stripe Connect payment system (for Creators), third-party cookies may be installed:
- **Stripe:** Necessary for fraud prevention and secure management of payments and royalties.
- **GitHub:** Necessary for the proper functioning of OAuth login.

Please refer to the respective Privacy Policies of Stripe and GitHub for more details.
