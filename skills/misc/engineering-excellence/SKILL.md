## Quality Gates Checklist
Before completing any coding task, ensure the following principles (as detailed in the README) are respected:
- ✅ **Test-Driven Development (TDD):** Are tests written and passing?
- ✅ **SOLID & Clean Code:** Is the code maintainable, decoupled, and DRY?
- ✅ **Security by Design:** Are inputs sanitized and secrets safe?
- ✅ **Performance & Accessibility:** Are best practices respected without regressions?
- ✅ **Production Readiness:** Are CI/CD, i18n, and environment boundaries respected?
